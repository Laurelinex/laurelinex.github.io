Laureline Vaucoy

Full-Stack Software Developer

You might suspect it from my name; I’m originally from France. I left to study Linguistics abroad when I was 18 with the University of Poitiers in France, the University of Technology in Sydney, Australia and the University of Nanjing in China, graduating in Translation Studies. I picked up a few languages along the way like Mandarin Chinese, English and more recently Python, JavaScript, Java, HTML and CSS.

After my Masters at the University of Sheffield in Communication, I worked for nearly three years as a Project Manager in the localisation industry, combining my language skills with my time-management and organisational skills to deliver localised media content for TV and Video-On-Demand platforms across the world.

I decided to move away from the language sector and relocated to Scotland to join the Professional Software Development course at CodeClan where I gained thousands of hours’ worth of hands-on coding experience. I got to build tracking apps, games, and even a food ordering app implementing Stripe. I absolutely love the challenge and the creative thinking that building apps involves.
