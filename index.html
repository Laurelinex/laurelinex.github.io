About Me

Full-Stack Developer

Background

Originally from France, I've studied Linguistics in the UK, with additional research in Australia and China, before working as a Project Manager for media localisation. Now I build software from Scotland.

Career

I transitioned into tech through rigorous training and have spent 5+ years shipping production-grade applications from the defense industry to web/mobile agency work, collaborating directly with clients to turn needs into scalable technical solutions.

Development

My technical expertise centers on React and Blazor applications. I operate equally well in greenfield prototyping and legacy refactoring. I ship robust solutions without over-engineering, but never compromise on security or maintainability.

Interests

I'm passionate about indie gaming, particularly immersive RPGs like Kingdom Come: Deliverance, my aboslute favourite. When not on a PC, I enjoy traveling and exploring new cuisines. Next stop when circumstances permit will be the Czech Republic.
